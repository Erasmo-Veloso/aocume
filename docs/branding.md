# Branding

## Brand Overview

AOCUME represents professionalism, international business, trust, and business growth.

The company bridges Angolan entrepreneurs with Chinese manufacturers through consulting, sourcing, importation, and professional guidance.

The visual identity should communicate credibility before anything else.

---

# Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

- Professional
- Reliable
- Modern
- Approachable
- Experienced
- Practical
- International
- Confident

---

# Brand Attributes

AOCUME is:

- Trustworthy
- Business-focused
- Educational
- Strategic
- Efficient
- Transparent

AOCUME is not:

- Playful
- Informal
- Luxury-focused
- Corporate bureaucratic
- Overly technical

---

# Mission

Empower entrepreneurs and businesses by simplifying international sourcing and importation through professional consulting and practical education.

---

# Vision

Become one of Angola's leading references in international trade consulting, professional importation services, and business education.

---

# Core Values

- Trust
- Integrity
- Commitment
- Transparency
- Professionalism
- Continuous Learning
- Customer Success

---

# Tone of Voice

Communication should always be:

- Clear
- Direct
- Friendly
- Professional
- Educational
- Encouraging

Avoid:

- Technical jargon
- Aggressive marketing
- Exaggerated promises
- Informal slang

---

# Logo Usage

The logo should always have sufficient spacing around it.

Never:

- Stretch
- Rotate
- Distort
- Change proportions
- Add effects
- Change colors

---

# Color Palette

## Primary

Black

Purpose:

- Typography
- Navigation
- Main buttons
- Brand identity

Approximation:

#0B0B0B

---

## Secondary

Golden Yellow

Purpose:

- CTA buttons
- Highlights
- Links
- Icons
- Decorative elements

Approximation:

#F4B731

---

## Background

White

Approximation:

#FFFFFF

---

## Neutral

Gray

Purpose:

- Borders
- Secondary text
- Inputs

Approximation:

#E5E5E5

---

# Typography

Primary Font

Poppins

Fallback

sans-serif

Reasons:

- Modern
- Clean
- Highly readable
- Professional appearance

---

# Iconography

Use simple outline icons.

Preferred libraries:

- Lucide
- Heroicons

Icons should be minimal and consistent.

---

# Photography

Use authentic images whenever possible.

Priority:

- Santiago Mulonga
- Real clients
- Containers
- Factories
- Chinese suppliers
- Products
- Business meetings
- Importation process

Avoid generic stock photos whenever possible.

---

# Buttons

Primary

Black background

White text

Golden hover accent

---

Secondary

White background

Black border

Black text

---

CTA

Golden background

Black text

High visual prominence.

---

# Visual Style

The interface should prioritize:

- Large whitespace
- Strong typography
- Rounded corners
- Soft shadows
- Minimal decorations
- High readability

The design should communicate professionalism rather than complexity.
